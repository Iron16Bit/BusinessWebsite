What is "Smart Stoves"?

Smart stoves is a project made up of two products:

  • Smart Cookware
  • Mobile App

    • The aim of these products is to provide cooking solutions to students and workers who don't have enough time to spend cooking or simply don't enjoy it.

    Smart Cookware

    • Automated temperature control
    • Multi-compartment cooking
    • Self-cleaning
    • Integrated sensors for precision

    App

    • Fight waste and lack of ideas! Based on the ingredients you have at home, the app can suggest you recipes that only need those ingredients or few more
    • You struggle to think about what to cook for a whole week in advance? The app offers a weekly meals planner, also integrated with a sopping list
    • The app allows you to keep track of what you eat and achieve a more balanced diet
    • Even if you aren't a pro in the kitchen, the app's interactive step-by-step cooking assistance will guide you during the meal preparation

    How It Works

    Connect the smart cookware to the app, schedule your meals, and let the system do the rest. The app guides you step-by-step to create delicious and balanced meals with ease.
