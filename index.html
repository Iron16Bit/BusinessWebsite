App

Our application makes food selection safe for people with allergies or dietary restrictions.
All you have to do is scan the barcode of a product and you will immediately know if it's safe for you. If it's not, you will get personalized suggestions for allergy-friendly products with similar characteristics.

Our main features are:

  • Quickly scan product barcodes to identify allergens
  • Get an immediate response on whether the product contains ingredients you should avoid.
  • Discover alternative products with similar characteristics but free from your restricted ingredients
  • Tailor the app to your unique dietary needs by selecting allergens or intolerances.
  • Discover our incredible meal planner to help you plan weekly meals in a balanced way.
