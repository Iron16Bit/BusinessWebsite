What is "Smart Stoves"?

Smart stoves is a project made up of two products:

  • Smart Cookware
  • Mobile App

    • The aim of these products is to provide cooking solutions to students and workers who don't have enough time to spend cooking or simply don't enjoy it.

    Smart Cookware

    • No time for cooking a meal you would actually enjoy? Just put in the ingredients and the Smart Cookware will take care of the rest
    • An AI works together with a set of integrated sensors to ensure your meals are prepared perfectly
    • The detachable structure of the Smart Cookware makes it really easy to clean
    • Together with the app: suggests new recipes, takes care of your meal planning and tracks your diet

    App

    • Fight waste and lack of ideas! Based on the ingredients you have at home, the app can suggest you recipes that only need those ingredients or few more
    • You struggle to think about what to cook for a whole week in advance? The app offers a weekly meals planner, also integrated with a shopping list
    • The app allows you to keep track of what you eat and achieve a more balanced diet. You can even personalize like by only having gluten-free or vegan meals
    • Even if you aren't a pro in the kitchen, the app's interactive step-by-step cooking assistance will guide you during the meal preparation
